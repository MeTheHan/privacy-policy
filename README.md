Privacy Policy for MTechno YouTube Subscription Check

Last Updated: [Date]

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Google Authentication Data

When you use Google Sign-In to verify your YouTube subscription, we collect:

Your Google account email address
Your Google account display name
YouTube channel subscription status
Google OAuth access token (temporary)
1.2 YouTube Data

We access your YouTube data to verify subscription status:

Your YouTube channel subscriptions
Channel membership status for MTechno channel
2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Purpose of Data Collection

We use your information solely to:

Verify your subscription to the MTechno YouTube channel
Provide access to exclusive content for subscribers
Authenticate your identity through Google Sign-In
2.2 Data Processing

Subscription verification happens in real-time
We do not store your YouTube data permanently
Google OAuth tokens are used temporarily and not stored
3. Data Storage and Retention

3.1 Storage Duration

Google authentication data: Not stored
YouTube subscription data: Not stored
Session data: Until browser session ends
3.2 Data Location

All data processing occurs through:

Google Firebase Authentication
YouTube Data API v3
No personal data is stored on our servers
4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Google Services

We use:

Google OAuth 2.0 for authentication
YouTube Data API v3 for subscription verification
Firebase for authentication services
4.2 Data Sharing

We do not:

Sell your data to third parties
Share your personal information
Use your data for marketing purposes
5. User Rights

5.1 Your Control

You have the right to:

Withdraw Google OAuth consent at any time
Delete your Google account connection
Access what data we process
5.2 Revoking Access

To revoke our access:

Visit Google Account Security settings
Go to "Third-party apps with account access"
Remove "MTechno YouTube Subscription Check"
6. Security

6.1 Protection Measures

All data transmitted via HTTPS encryption
Google OAuth 2.0 security protocols
No sensitive data storage on our side
7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
